## TL;DR
Hack The Boo 2025 was a Halloween-themed CTF hosted by Hack The Box.
Here's my experience participating through the HTB Kigali meetup group.

## About the Event
Hack The Boo is HTB's annual Halloween CTF featuring spooky-themed
challenges across multiple categories. The 2025 edition drew 92
attendees from 118 groups worldwide including our Kigali chapter.

## Categories
- Web
- Pwn
- Reversing
- Crypto
- Forensics
- OSINT

## Approach
CTFs like Hack The Boo are great for practicing under time pressure.
My strategy:

1. **Triage first** — scan all challenges quickly, start with the
ones worth the most points in categories you're strongest in
2. **Don't tunnel** — if stuck for 30 minutes, move on and come back
3. **Document everything** — even failed attempts help with writeups

## Key Lessons
- Time management matters more than raw skill in CTFs
- Read challenge descriptions carefully — hints are often hidden there
- Team collaboration multiplies what you can solve solo
