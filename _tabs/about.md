## What I Do

I work in cybersecurity as a professional while spending my off-hours doing what I actually love — breaking things, finding bugs, and building knowledge in public.

My work spans both sides of the fence:

- **Red team** — machine walkthroughs, CTF competitions, vulnerability research, and CVE hunting
- **Blue team** — threat detection, SIEM analysis, and understanding how defenders think

I believe the best security professionals understand both perspectives. Offense informs defense. You can't protect what you don't understand how to break.
